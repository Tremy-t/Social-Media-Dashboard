Our Story

Founded in 2015, Brew Haven began with a simple mission: create a space where coffee, community, and comfort intersect. Our founders, Emily and James, met in culinary school and shared a passion for ethical sourcing and artisanal methods.

Today, we continue to source our beans directly from small farms around the world, roast in small batches, and serve with care. Our pastries are made fresh daily, and we're proud to support local producers for all our ingredients.
