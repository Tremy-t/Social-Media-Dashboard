Our Story

Mountain Brew began in 2010 with a simple idea: create a space where coffee isn't just served, but celebrated. Founded by two friends with a passion for quality coffee, we've grown from a small corner shop to a beloved local establishment.

We believe that great coffee brings people together. That's why we source only the finest beans, roast them with care, and serve each cup with pride. Our café is designed to be a welcoming space for everyone—whether you're catching up with friends, working remotely, or simply enjoying a moment of solitude.
